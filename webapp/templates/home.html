Welcome to Keep on Track

Keep on Track provides advanced asset tracking technology to help support East African smallholder farmers. By decreasing their losses, analyzing data, we aim to increase production productivity, which will have a number of positive effects on the regional economy. An increase in production of resources can lead to a decrease in poverty hunger and gender inequality. Our design utilizes an integrated softare and hardware solution that satisfies the needs of all our stakeholders. We use a Raspberry Pi with GPS and SIM modules to retrieve location data. This data, along with other various commands, are sent to our Google Cloud server where we process these requests to generate real-time messages and a map of the path of the working asset(s).
